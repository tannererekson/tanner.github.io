Personal Interests

One thing that I am really interested in is skiing. I only started to ski a year ago. I now have two years under my belt. I usually just get a season pass to Sundance because it is close and cheaper than most resorts. I have started to do 360's off of jumps but my goal for next year will be to do a backflip.

Another thing that I really enjoy doing is resaling golf clubs. This is a hobby that a picked up last year also. I usually just look on the KSL classifieds page to try and find good deals on golf clubs. I then clean the clubs up and post them on different websites to try and sale them. It has been a good side business for me while I try and make it though college while being recently married.

Watching movies is also a fun way to pass time. Currently, my wife and I are watching all of the Marvel movies in chronological order. There are over 20 movies to watch but we are down to the last 4. Some of my favorite movies include: The Italian Job, UHF, Napolean Dynamite, and World War Z.
